--- ACCOUNTS & SYSTEM RULES ---

• FICA ACCOUNT (Light Blue Circle)
- Target Cap: $75,000
- Purpose: Acts as the primary financial safety net.
- Rule: This balance is calculated specifically to cover 6 months of normal household expenses plus all insurance policy deductibles.

• INVESTMENT ACCOUNT (Dark Blue Circle)
- Balance: $15,000+
- Purpose: Generates long-term wealth growth.
- Rule: Once the FICA Account is fully funded at its target cap ($75,000), any remaining surplus money ("Remainder") cascades directly into this account.

• AUTOMATED REBALANCING (Bi-directional Arrow)
- System Logic: A double-headed arrow connects these two accounts. Money flows dynamically between them to maintain the FICA account's safety floor while continuously deploying excess capital into investment strategies.
